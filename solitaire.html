Rules

The objective is to remove all the marbles from the board except one, by making valid moves according to the following rules:/p>

  • 1: Player starts off by removing the centre piece.
  • 2: Select a piece and jump over the adjacent one into a vacant hole.
  • 3: Never jump diagonally, always horizontally or vertically.
  • 4: The ‘jumped over’ piece is removed from the board.
  • 5: The player repeats this move until there are no more pieces left to jump over.
  • 6: The game is over when the player cannot jump over any of the other pieces.
  • 7: The player can only finish a game successfully, by landing in the centre hole with the final move.
